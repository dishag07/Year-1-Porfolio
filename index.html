Disha Gupta is a Sophomore student attending Marlboro Highschool. She is fluent in both English and Hindi, and has spent five plus years learning Spanish. On Friday evenings she volunteers her time teaching young children Hindi at the Holmdel Hindi school; where she graduated from. Disha has a younger sister, named Anisha, and a dog. She is helpful, ambitious, and kind. She stands against bullying and is a strong advocate for women's rights. Despite her age, she is a skilled crochter and a strong reader. In her free time, Disha writes collaborative pieces in groups of other young, and talented writers. She dedicates herself to her grades, and family. She lacks in experience but has all the makings for an excellent employee.
